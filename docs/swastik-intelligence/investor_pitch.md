# Investor Pitch: Swastik Medicare
**Gorakhpur's Integrated Healthcare Ecosystem**

## 1. Problem
- **Accessibility & Verifiability**: People in Tier-2 cities struggle to find verified doctors and genuine medicines.
- **Fragmentation**: No unified platform currently exists that bridges the gap between doctors, hospitals, insurance, and patients in cities like Gorakhpur.

## 2. Solution
**Swastik Medicare** is an all-in-one platform providing:
- **Verified Doctor Directory** with video consultation.
- **Retailer & Pharmacy Network** for rapid medicine delivery.
- **Integrated Insurance & Ambulance services**.
- **Agent CRM** for high-touch local partner onboarding.

## 3. Market
- **Focus**: Starting with **Gorakhpur** (20+ Lakh population).
- **Scalability**: Designed for 100+ Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities in India.

## 4. Revenue Model
- **Lead Generation Fees**: From hospitals and specialist doctors.
- **Subscription Listing**: Monthly fees for premium provider visibility.
- **Commissions**: On medicine orders and insurance conversions.
- **Ads & Featured Placement**: Local clinical visibility.

## 5. Traction
- **Platform Ready**: Full CRM, Partner Portals, and Payment gateway integrated.
- **Partner Network**: Scaling to 100+ doctors and 50+ pharmacies in Gorakhpur.
- **Lead Flow**: Ready-to-scale bulk outreach systems via WhatsApp Automation.

## 6. The Ask
- **Funding**: Raising **₹50 Lakh** for scaling operations, region-wide marketing, and recruitment of local sales agents.
